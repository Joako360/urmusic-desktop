Urmusic is a fun app to create and share your own music visualizers, easily and for free. The desktop version is made in Java using FFmpeg, JTransforms, GSON and MP3SPI.

FFmpeg: http://ffmpeg.org
JTransforms: https://sites.google.com/site/piotrwendykier/software/jtransforms
GSON: https://github.com/google/gson
MP3SPI: http://www.javazoom.net/mp3spi/mp3spi.html
